Our approach to confronting the Covid-19

We will put the safety of all Main users at the heart of our business all the time. We are continuing to expand our response to COVID-19 by providing new features to our users, and by providing support to all of our donors, contributions, and initiatives in the interest of our community.

We will support Governmental entities. The safety of all Main users is our top priority at all times, so we are committed to supporting the Governmental entities in their efforts to end COVID-19 pandemic. And work to vaccinate all employees of the company, and no practitioner or service provider will be accepted in Main until making sure that the service provider complete the dose of the vaccination.
