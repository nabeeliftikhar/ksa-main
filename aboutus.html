Our story

Starting From Scratch

The end of 2018 was the beginning of the emergence of an idea to find a solution to an existing problem that many society suffers from, which is the provision of home health services professionally. This idea continued in the process of research, crystallization and formulation until the beginning of the Corona pandemic, which was the great reason for launching this important and vital project, which made us believe in the care and service that will be provided in a safe environment free of infection and pollutants, As we were keen to build our name and identity with deliberate steps that reflect the quality of service according to our global standards that no one has preceded us to apply in the Middle East, to reach these standards and indicators to a prominent position among one of the most important leading companies in this field,We will develop the concept of service in order to suit the requirements and aspirations of the client accurately. This will only be with specialists who are the beginning of our success story and the basis of our continuity and excellence. We continue with you to tell our story and creativity in order to achieve the goal of happiness for all.

We are not innovating the quality of service, we are innovating the procedures we provide through service

- Talal

Our Vision

Main 2030 Vision

Main Home Health Care Services, its activities, and events are derived from Vision 2030, and is based on supporting the achievement of its goals by providing pioneering, quality home health services, and creating an incubating and enabling environment for home care, With a focus on innovative development goals to work on, in accordance with global education and training based on the latest methodologies, techniques and methods. Therefore; Main is committed to supporting the ambitious Saudi Vision 2030 launched by His Royal Highness Prince Mohammed bin Salman bin Abdulaziz Al Saud, Crown Prince to reach a vibrant and active society that provides everyone with a decent and happy life, a sustainable lifestyle, social solidarity, effective health and social care system. To support innovation and creative ideas in providing home health services.
  • Healthcare is a key component of Vision 2030, and Main's mission is to provide innovative home health ideas, with the ability to exploit opportunities in the Saudi economy.
  • Main offers the best international medical evaluations and standards, to provide unique services that meet the needs of the Saudi community, as we seek to enable a full and healthy independent life in a safe and infection-free home environment.
  • Main will contribute to bridging the gap between the services provided in home care and the needs of the individual within his home by providing high quality home services, training, education, and educational and cultural materials to all beneficiaries and service providers alike.
  • Main will be the link between the beneficiaries of home services that revolve around leadership, innovation and creativity in providing home services, and its providers of qualified specialists, investors, and a wide variety of international and local agencies interested in pioneering innovation in the provision of home health services.
  • Main works to connect beneficiaries and providers of home services with changes in information technology, new assessment and diagnostic models, integration, safety and sustainability in their provision, and to prepare a generation of home service providers equipped with all skills; to achieve success in the home health care environment.
  • Main is designed to meet stringent quality and safety standards, with the aim of achieving international accreditations for providing home health services, in addition to the local accreditation standards of the Saudi Center for Accreditation of Health Facilities.

Our Values

Our Values Defines Who We Really Are

Focus On The Beneficiary

We put the client and caregiver experience at the heart of everything we do by providing client- centered care. We empower clients and caregivers to take care of their own health through education, training, encouragement and ongoing education.

Integrity

Our decisions are based on high ethical standards. To make sure that our actions match our words.

Responsibility

We are committed to exceeding the expectations of high quality for the beneficiary and caregivers individually and in an organized manner, taking it upon ourselves to bear all our actions and results, and we can be relied upon to fulfill our commitments and services clearly.

Partnership

We believe and committed to working collaboratively to achieve the best results and successes.

Work as One Team

Success can only be achieved through the coordinated and collaborative efforts of all. In order for employees to feel a sense of belonging and to be inspired to contribute to achieving organizational success.

Transperancy

We are open and deal with high transparency in our communications and work.

Respect

We acknowledge that respect is a basic approach and a pillar of work, service provision and dealing inside and outside the organization.

Innovation

It encourages disciplined creative thinking with the highest standards of safety and security in the procedure, especially with regard to health services provided to clients.
